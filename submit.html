Have something you've been working on? Want to share it with the UMass community?

You can submit up to:

  • 3 pieces of Fiction
  • 3 pieces of Nonfiction/Critcism
  • 6 Poems
  • Unlimited Media!
Media can be any form: photography, painting, mixed media, sculpting, graphic art, drawings etc. Take a pic and send it over!

Guidelines

  • Title the piece
  • Do not put any personal information in the document
  • Keep submissions up to 2,500 words
  • Submit literature as a PDF
  • Submit media as a high-quality JPG

Send all submissions to umassjabberwocky@gmail.com!
